Tarot Fabula

Like many stories, the images on a tarot card can tell a unique narrative when placed in a particular pattern.
Make your own story with the image cues given by each or every card.
Re-shuffle the deck as often as you like for a new set of story elements, themes, and characters.
Interpret freely given the cues on the modals.
For more info on the origin of these cards, head to the collection.
