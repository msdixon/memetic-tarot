About Tarot Fabula

a capstone project examining tarot card symbolism, story serendipity, and playful potentiality, shuffled

Tarot Fabula was created in fulfillment of an MA in Digital Humanities at The Graduate Center @ CUNY. This is a research project and website that examines the digital tarot card deck as a unique narrative form, using the digital archives of card decks out of their original context to define and reshape meaning, playfully.

Tarot Fabula interrogates the pattern of digital applications that provide interactions (such as shuffling and card presentation) that mimic the interactions of using a physical tarot card deck, and interrogates the meaning-making created by randomness.

In Tarot Fabula, randomness is designed and has limitations, utilizing a programmatic solution like the Fisher-Yates shuffling algorithm to shuffle through a limited number of several cards with different designs and similar values.

In thinking about how machine learning algorithms, search engines, media recommendation tools, and consumer “personalization” of advertisements all utilize some sense of controlled mechanical randomness, a project like Tarot Fabula lays its cards bare: This set upends the expectations of an experienced tarot user though it is using a hidden mechanism to choose cards.

The Tarot Fabula project asks if the explicit digital shuffling of a dataset of card images feels different than hands shuffling a 78-card deck.

I interrogated this with an assumption of what tarot readings can appear to do: by providing the images in a certain presentation, can random cards tell a tarot querant a story?

Can the story presented to the querant by the cards as presented change when using different tarot decks, multiple cards, other pattern-breaking surprises? Can viewing a unique reading like this create new meanings for previously-understood archetypes presented in some tarot images, and does this open up more narrative possibilities? Can the mechanic that is presented as randomness itself be trusted?

Importantly, Tarot Fabula is a practice in expansive possibilities, in the potentiality that is otherwise lost to linear narratives, ordered decks, rigid design, querying fate.

In Tarot Fabula, we look to the tarot system's rich past in order to better design our own stories of the future, in the present's era of algorithms and unreliable information.
